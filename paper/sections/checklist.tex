    Question: Do the main claims made in the abstract and introduction
    accurately reflect the paper's contributions and scope?\\
    Answer: \answerYes{}\\
    Justification: The abstract enumerates the four headline empirical claims

    Section~\ref{sec:experiments} with sample sizes and significance tests.

    Question: Does the paper discuss the limitations of the work performed by
